<p> # Understanding Self-Attention
## What is Self-Attention?
Self-attention is a fundamental concept in deep learning, particularly in Natural Language Processing (NLP) models. It's designed to capture long-range dependencies and relationships within sequential data.

*   **Problem of sequential data**: When dealing with sequential data like text or time series, it can be challenging to model the dependencies between consecutive elements. Traditional approaches often rely on recurrent neural networks (RNNs) or transformers, which have limitations in capturing complex dependencies.</p>
